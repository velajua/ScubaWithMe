# ScubaWithMe Story Notes

This folder holds the narrative system for the ScubaWithMe daily character content.

The story direction is built around a small reef community learning how to understand divers without losing their wildness, boundaries, or reef identity. The goal is to create attention-friendly daily posts that still support the larger photography brand: calm discovery, marine wonder, respectful observation, and a personal invitation into the underwater world.

## Files

- `series-bible.md`: full premise, emotional arc, story rules, and long-term narrative.
- `characters.md`: main cast, recurring one-off characters, dynamics, and running jokes.
- `story-arcs.md`: season-scale arcs and adventure plots.
- `episode-engines.md`: repeatable story engines, filler prompts, and daily 4-frame formulas.
- `daily-workflow.md`: practical workflow for turning the story into daily posts.

## Core Idea

The reef starts afraid of divers because divers can be noisy, bright, clumsy, and too close. Over time, Turtle, Grumpy Fish, Shark, and Lionfish learn to tell the difference between careless visitors and respectful observers.

The story should never teach that marine life exists to entertain humans. It should teach that if a visitor enters gently, the ocean may let them witness something real.
