# ScubaWithMe Brand Design

## Goal

Create a usable brand identity for ScubaWithMe that supports Shopify setup without drifting away from the photography-led direction defined in [AGENTS.md](/C:/Users/juanv/Downloads/ScubaWithMe/AGENTS.md).

## Chosen Direction

The selected direction is `Coastal Signature`.

This identity uses:

- a slightly stylized but clearly anatomical sea turtle
- restrained natural bubbles as an underwater accent
- a relaxed script wordmark that feels personal and beach-adjacent
- the primary logo text integrated across the turtle shell
- calm ocean/seafoam/sand tones taken from the project photography

This identity avoids:

- geometric doodle turtles
- mascot/cartoon dive-shop branding
- loud tropical gradients
- fake luxury gallery styling

## Logo System

The brand system includes:

- `Primary logo`: shell-integrated turtle logo for the storefront header and main branding
- `Secondary logo`: centered square presentation of the shell-integrated logo for square placements
- `Wordmark`: text-only version for optional tight headers or overlay use
- `Icon`: turtle-only square badge for favicon/social/avatar usage
- `Watermark`: quiet mark for previewing photos and mockups

## Visual Rules

### Turtle mark

- Must read as a real sea turtle at a glance
- Must keep forward glide and calm posture
- Must not collapse into circles/lines/doodle geometry
- Should feel outline-led and slightly cartoonish rather than realistic
- Must stay simple enough for small-size recognition

### Bubbles

- Keep only a few small bubbles near the head
- Bubbles should feel natural, not decorative filler
- Remove bubbles from the smallest favicon if they hurt clarity

### Wordmark

- Script-led, warm, and relaxed
- Should feel personal rather than corporate
- In the main logo, the lettering should sit on the shell
- Must remain legible when reduced for store header use

## Color Direction

Primary palette:

- `Ocean Dark`: deep header/footer anchor
- `Ocean`: main brand color
- `Ocean Mid`: secondary UI/header accent
- `Seafoam`: accent/UI highlight
- `Seafoam Light`: board/background support
- `Sand`: warm neutral pulled from turtle shell and reef warmth

## Constraints

- Preserve project photography as the lead visual identity
- Do not claim these assets are print proofs
- Do not overwrite source imagery
- Keep the outcome usable for Shopify setup, Instagram, and sample mockups
