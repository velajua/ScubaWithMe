# Below With Me: Series Overview

## Premise

`Below With Me` is a daily underwater micro-comic world for ScubaWithMe.

The reef is a small neighborhood near a dive path. The animals know divers by their bubbles, lights, fins, and awkward hovering. Some divers rush. Some kick sand. Some get too close. So the reef has learned to be suspicious.

Then a quieter diver keeps returning. This diver moves slowly, watches from a respectful distance, and does not touch the reef. Turtle notices first. The rest of the crew argues about whether the quiet bubble is dangerous, annoying, interesting, or maybe different.

The full story follows the reef community as they move from fear to caution, from caution to curiosity, and from curiosity to a kind of respectful coexistence.

## Main Cast

- Turtle: calm guide, slow wisdom, emotional center.
- Grumpy Fish: skeptical, funny, blunt, secretly protective.
- Shark: misunderstood local, calm but intimidating-looking.
- Lionfish: dramatic, elegant, proud, beautiful from a distance.
- The Silent Diver: mostly offscreen viewer stand-in, shown through bubbles, camera, fins, shadow, and restraint.

## Long-Term Emotional Arc

1. The reef starts suspicious of divers.
2. Turtle is curious but cautious.
3. Grumpy Fish assumes all divers are rude bubble machines.
4. Shark is tired of being treated like a monster.
5. Lionfish likes admiration but rejects closeness.
6. The animals learn that careless visitors and respectful observers are not the same.
7. The diver becomes a recurring silent presence.
8. The animals learn to interact through boundaries, distance, patience, and recognition.
9. The reef becomes less afraid, but never tame.
10. The final message becomes: "If you enter gently, the ocean lets you witness."

## Story Spine

The story moves from:

- `Divers are scary.`
- `Some visitors disturb. Some visitors witness.`
- `The reef does not perform for humans, but it can be seen by those who respect it.`

## Tone

Use:

- warm curiosity
- dry gentle humor
- tiny ocean truths
- calm adventure
- respectful observation
- recurring jokes
- character personality

Avoid:

- loud mascot comedy
- childish punchlines as the default
- preachy conservation speeches
- fake marine facts
- selling every post
- making the diver the hero
- making the animals pets

## Story Rules

- The animals stay wild.
- The diver does not touch, feed, grab, ride, chase, or casually rescue marine life.
- Interaction means respectful observation.
- The human is mostly offscreen so the viewer feels like the diver.
- The reef has its own life whether humans are present or not.
- Humor comes from misunderstanding, personality, and reef life.
- Every few posts should carry one tiny real ocean truth.
- Boundaries are a core theme, especially with Lionfish, Shark, Pufferfish, Moray, and shy reef animals.

## Brand Fit

This story supports ScubaWithMe because it turns the brand into an invitation: come see the underwater world with me, slowly and respectfully.

The cartoons can catch attention, but they should support the underwater photography brand rather than replace it.
