# Arc: Night Dive

## Premise

The same reef becomes a different world after dark.

## Main Uses

- mystery
- glowing visuals
- shadow humor
- night-dive education
- showing another side of each character

## Plot Points

- Familiar coral becomes strange.
- Jellyfish and glowing plankton appear.
- Shark becomes calmer at night.
- Grumpy Fish hates everything after sunset.
- Lionfish becomes extra dramatic under dive lights.
- Turtle explains that the ocean has many moods.
- The quiet diver returns for a night dive.
- The diver's light reveals details without chasing them.

## Possible Ending

The reef is not one place. It changes with light, time, and attention.

## 8-Episode Outline

### Episode 1: The Reef Lowers Its Voice

Lead: Turtle

4-frame outline:

1. The sun fades and the reef darkens.
2. Familiar coral becomes silhouette.
3. Grumpy Fish says the reef is being suspicious.
4. Turtle says, "It is only speaking softer."

Final turn: mood shift.

### Episode 2: Night Complaints

Lead: Grumpy Fish

4-frame outline:

1. Grumpy Fish peers from a dark crevice.
2. A tiny glowing speck passes by.
3. He says the dark is showing off.
4. Shark says, "You said that about noon."

Final turn: recurring grumpiness.

### Episode 3: Shark In The Quiet

Lead: Shark

4-frame outline:

1. Shark glides calmly through dim blue water.
2. Turtle notices he seems different.
3. Shark says the night makes fewer assumptions.
4. Grumpy Fish says, "That is beautiful and upsetting."

Final turn: depth with humor.

### Episode 4: Jellyfish Glow

Lead: Jellyfish

4-frame outline:

1. Jellyfish drifts through faint glow.
2. Lionfish asks who designed the lighting.
3. Jellyfish says, "The dark remembers."
4. Turtle watches in silence.

Final turn: wonder.

### Episode 5: Drama Under The Beam

Lead: Lionfish

4-frame outline:

1. The quiet diver's light moves slowly.
2. Lionfish appears in the beam.
3. The diver keeps respectful distance.
4. Lionfish says, "At last, a spotlight with manners."

Final turn: beauty plus boundaries.

### Episode 6: The Sand Speaks

Lead: Stingray

4-frame outline:

1. A shape rises from the sand.
2. Grumpy Fish yells.
3. Stingray blinks calmly.
4. Stingray says, "I was here the whole time. You were just loud."

Final turn: side character reveal.

### Episode 7: Light Can Listen

Lead: Turtle

4-frame outline:

1. The diver's light catches a small creature.
2. The creature turns away.
3. The diver moves the light away.
4. Turtle says, "Even light can listen."

Final turn: respectful observation.

### Episode 8: Morning Proof

Lead: Group

4-frame outline:

1. Morning light returns.
2. The reef looks familiar again.
3. Grumpy Fish says the night exaggerated everything.
4. Turtle says, "No. It introduced itself."

Final turn: wonder and closure.
