# Season 1: The Bubble Stranger

## Purpose

Introduce the reef, the main cast, and the first emotional question: are divers always dangerous, or does behavior matter?

Minimum length: 8 episodes.

## Episode 1: The Bubble Alarm

Lead: Grumpy Fish

Purpose: Start with fear and comedy.

4-frame outline:

1. Strange bubbles rise over the reef.
2. Grumpy Fish declares an emergency.
3. Turtle says to watch before hiding.
4. Shark passes by and says, "I am being blamed for a lot less than this."

Final turn: funny fear, first hint that assumptions are messy.

## Episode 2: Watch The Movement

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Establish Turtle's observation-first role.

4-frame outline:

1. The diver appears in the distance.
2. Grumpy Fish calls them a bubble machine.
3. Turtle notices the diver is not chasing anything.
4. Turtle says, "Shape tells one story. Movement tells another."

Final turn: quiet lesson.

## Episode 3: This Is Just My Face

Lead: Shark

Purpose: Introduce Shark as misunderstood.

4-frame outline:

1. Shark sees the diver's camera turn toward him.
2. Shark ducks behind coral and fails because he is too large.
3. Grumpy Fish says hiding is not his strongest skill.
4. Shark says, "This is just my face. Everyone keeps making it a headline."

Final turn: humor plus empathy.

## Episode 4: Admire From There

Lead: Lionfish

Purpose: Introduce beauty and boundaries.

4-frame outline:

1. Lionfish glides into nice light.
2. The diver pauses and raises the camera.
3. Lionfish enjoys the attention until the diver drifts closer.
4. The diver backs away, and Lionfish says, "Finally, an audience with manners."

Final turn: distance as respect.

## Episode 5: The Sandstorm

Lead: Group

Purpose: Show why the reef is suspicious.

4-frame outline:

1. A careless diver kicks sand across the reef.
2. Grumpy Fish disappears in a cloud of sand.
3. Turtle waits for the water to clear.
4. Grumpy Fish says, "I have become weather."

Final turn: funny, but with real disturbance.

## Episode 6: The Quiet Bubble Returns

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Separate the respectful diver from the careless one.

4-frame outline:

1. Another diver-shaped shadow appears.
2. Grumpy Fish prepares a complaint.
3. Turtle notices the slow hover and careful fins.
4. Turtle says, "This is the quiet bubble."

Final turn: recognition begins.

## Episode 7: Photobomb

Lead: Grumpy Fish

Purpose: Let suspicion become accidental engagement.

4-frame outline:

1. The diver frames a calm reef photo.
2. Grumpy Fish swims into the center by accident.
3. Shark asks if that was intentional.
4. Grumpy Fish says, "Obviously. The reef needed representation."

Final turn: shareable comedy.

## Episode 8: Not Trust, Notice

Lead: Group

Purpose: Close the first arc without making the animals naive.

4-frame outline:

1. The crew watches the diver leave without touching anything.
2. Grumpy Fish asks if they are friends now.
3. Turtle says friendship is too fast.
4. Shark says, "But behavior is evidence."

Final turn: the season thesis.
