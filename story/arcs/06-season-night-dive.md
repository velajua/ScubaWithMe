# Season 6: Night Dive

## Purpose

Show that the same reef becomes a different world after dark.

Minimum length: 8 episodes.

## Episode 1: The Reef Gets Quiet

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Establish a changed mood.

4-frame outline:

1. Light fades over the reef.
2. Familiar colors turn into shapes.
3. Grumpy Fish asks who approved nighttime.
4. Turtle says, "The reef has more than one voice."

Final turn: atmosphere.

## Episode 2: After-Sunset Complaint

Lead: Grumpy Fish

Purpose: Let Grumpy Fish carry night anxiety.

4-frame outline:

1. Grumpy Fish peers from a shadow.
2. A tiny glow passes by.
3. Grumpy Fish says the dark is doing too much.
4. Shark says, "You complain during daylight too."

Final turn: character joke.

## Episode 3: Calm In The Dark

Lead: Shark

Purpose: Reveal Shark is steadier at night.

4-frame outline:

1. Shark moves through a dark blue silhouette.
2. Turtle notices he seems calmer.
3. Shark says loud shapes are softer now.
4. Grumpy Fish says that is the most terrifying poetry he has heard.

Final turn: humor plus depth.

## Episode 4: The Glow Arrives

Lead: Jellyfish

Purpose: Bring in wonder.

4-frame outline:

1. Jellyfish drifts through faint glow.
2. Lionfish asks if the lighting is natural.
3. Jellyfish says the dark remembers how to shine.
4. Turtle says nothing, just watches.

Final turn: wonder.

## Episode 5: Lionfish Under Lights

Lead: Lionfish

Purpose: Use dive light beauty and boundaries.

4-frame outline:

1. The quiet diver's light passes slowly.
2. Lionfish glows in the beam.
3. The diver stops at a respectful distance.
4. Lionfish says, "The night understands drama."

Final turn: visual character beat.

## Episode 6: The Wrong Shadow

Lead: Group

Purpose: Use nighttime misreadings.

4-frame outline:

1. A large shadow crosses the sand.
2. Grumpy Fish declares a second emergency.
3. Stingray rises from the sand.
4. Stingray says, "I was here the whole time. You were just loud."

Final turn: side character joke.

## Episode 7: Light Without Chasing

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Show respectful night diving.

4-frame outline:

1. The quiet diver's light finds a small creature.
2. The creature turns away.
3. The light moves away too.
4. Turtle says, "Even light can listen."

Final turn: respect lesson.

## Episode 8: A Whole New Reef

Lead: Group

Purpose: Close with discovery.

4-frame outline:

1. Morning starts to return.
2. The crew sees the familiar reef again.
3. Grumpy Fish says it looked bigger in the dark.
4. Turtle says, "It was. We were seeing a different part of it."

Final turn: wonder.
