# Season 3: Current Season

## Purpose

Open the world beyond the home reef and turn current into a story force.

Minimum length: 8 episodes.

## Episode 1: Current Business

Lead: Grumpy Fish

Purpose: Make the current feel like a character.

4-frame outline:

1. The water starts pulling stronger than usual.
2. Grumpy Fish says he did not approve this schedule.
3. Turtle says currents have their own errands.
4. Shark says, "This is current business."

Final turn: running joke.

## Episode 2: The Long Drift

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Begin the separation.

4-frame outline:

1. Turtle follows a gentle pull.
2. The familiar coral thins out.
3. Turtle looks back and sees blue space.
4. Turtle says, "The long way just got longer."

Final turn: adventure begins.

## Episode 3: Jellyfish Advice

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Introduce Jellyfish as a drifting guide.

4-frame outline:

1. Turtle meets Jellyfish moving with the water.
2. Turtle asks where the current goes.
3. Jellyfish says the water has already made plans.
4. Turtle says, "Then I should probably start making some too."

Final turn: calm does not mean passive.

## Episode 4: Search Party Lighting

Lead: Lionfish

Purpose: Keep the home reef side funny.

4-frame outline:

1. The crew realizes Turtle is gone.
2. Grumpy Fish panics while denying panic.
3. Lionfish says every search party needs proper lighting.
4. Shark says, "We are finding Turtle, not opening a theater."

Final turn: group comedy.

## Episode 5: Shark Reads The Water

Lead: Shark

Purpose: Give Shark competence beyond the joke.

4-frame outline:

1. Shark watches sand trails and drifting bubbles.
2. Grumpy Fish asks if he is guessing.
3. Shark points toward the current.
4. Shark says, "Open water talks. It just does not use bubbles."

Final turn: wonder and competence.

## Episode 6: The Blue Hallway

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Give Turtle a poetic route name.

4-frame outline:

1. Turtle passes through a wide blue channel.
2. Small shapes appear and vanish below.
3. Turtle slows instead of fighting the current.
4. Turtle names it "the blue hallway."

Final turn: discovery.

## Episode 7: Found By The Shadow Garden

Lead: Group

Purpose: Reunite through place names and observation.

4-frame outline:

1. Shark leads the crew toward a darker coral edge.
2. Lionfish calls it terrible lighting.
3. Turtle appears from behind the coral.
4. Turtle says, "I was in the shadow garden."

Final turn: reunion with world-building.

## Episode 8: Bigger Than Home

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Close the arc with expanded world awareness.

4-frame outline:

1. The crew returns to the home reef.
2. Grumpy Fish says home is smaller than he remembered.
3. Shark says that is not the reef shrinking.
4. Turtle says, "That is us noticing more ocean."

Final turn: wonder.
