# Season 4: The Shiny Thing

## Purpose

Use a buried object as a mystery, comedy hook, and careful human-impact story.

Minimum length: 8 episodes.

## Episode 1: The Spark

Lead: Grumpy Fish

Purpose: Start the treasure mystery.

4-frame outline:

1. Grumpy Fish sees a glint in the sand.
2. He calls everyone over but says it is probably dangerous.
3. Lionfish says it is obviously treasure.
4. Shark says, "Treasure usually does not smell like boat."

Final turn: mystery hook.

## Episode 2: Royal Property

Lead: Crab

Purpose: Introduce object ownership comedy.

4-frame outline:

1. Crab climbs onto the shiny thing.
2. Crab declares it royal property.
3. Grumpy Fish asks who voted.
4. Crab says, "The crown did."

Final turn: side character comedy.

## Episode 3: The Stage Prop

Lead: Lionfish

Purpose: Let Lionfish project her personality onto the object.

4-frame outline:

1. Lionfish circles the shiny object.
2. She says it catches light beautifully.
3. Turtle asks whether it belongs here.
4. Lionfish says, "Belonging is complicated. Lighting is immediate."

Final turn: funny but thoughtful.

## Episode 4: Not All Stories Are Good

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Bring in the human-object theme carefully.

4-frame outline:

1. Turtle studies the buried edge.
2. Cleaner Shrimp says it came from above.
3. Grumpy Fish says above keeps dropping opinions.
4. Turtle says, "Old objects tell stories. Not all stories are good."

Final turn: reflective.

## Episode 5: Wrong Kind Of Treasure

Lead: Shark

Purpose: Reveal that the object may be trash or gear.

4-frame outline:

1. The sand shifts and more of the object appears.
2. Shark recognizes a human smell.
3. Lionfish asks if it is rare.
4. Shark says, "Rare does not mean welcome."

Final turn: boundary lesson.

## Episode 6: The Quiet Bubble Notices

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Bring the respectful diver back without making them heroic.

4-frame outline:

1. The quiet diver sees the object.
2. The reef crew hides and watches.
3. The diver marks or removes it carefully.
4. Grumpy Fish says, "Fine. One point for the bubble machine."

Final turn: cautious credit.

## Episode 7: Careless Or Curious

Lead: Group

Purpose: Let the crew debate humans without flattening the theme.

4-frame outline:

1. The object is gone or marked.
2. Lionfish says humans make strange offerings.
3. Shark says some drop things and some clean them.
4. Turtle says, "Both can be true. That is why we watch."

Final turn: nuance.

## Episode 8: The Sand Remembers

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Close with consequence and quiet restoration.

4-frame outline:

1. Sand slowly settles over the object's old place.
2. Small fish return to the patch.
3. Grumpy Fish says it looks almost normal.
4. Turtle says, "Almost is why we remember."

Final turn: gentle responsibility.
