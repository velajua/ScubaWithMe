# Episode 5: Flash In Sleepy Faces

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Introduce light sensitivity without overexplaining or making the tone preachy.

## 4-Frame Script

### Panel 1

Visual: A sleepy reef creature peeks from a coral nook in dim blue water.

Text: `Tiny nap happening.`

### Panel 2

Visual: A bright camera flash or dive light beam startles the creature.

Text: `FLASH`

### Panel 3

Visual: The creature retreats deeper into the coral while Grumpy Fish looks offended on its behalf.

Text: `Rude to the nap community.`

### Panel 4

Visual: Turtle watches the quiet space where the creature disappeared.

Text: `A good look should not cost someone their calm.`

## Final Turn

Quiet boundary: seeing something is not worth disturbing it.
