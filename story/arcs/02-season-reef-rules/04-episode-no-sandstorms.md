# Episode 4: No Sandstorms

Lead: Grumpy Fish

Purpose: Turn a real dive problem into a comic rule the reef can repeat.

## 4-Frame Script

### Panel 1

Visual: Grumpy Fish rests in a small hiding spot near the sand.

Text: `Finally. Peace.`

### Panel 2

Visual: A cloud of sand rolls through from off-panel, pushed by careless fin marks.

Text: `FWOOM`

### Panel 3

Visual: Grumpy Fish emerges covered in sand, eyes huge and furious.

Text: `I have become weather.`

### Panel 4

Visual: Turtle points calmly at the fin marks in the sand while Grumpy Fish glares.

Text: `Rule three: feet are not weather.`

## Final Turn

Funny rule with real reef etiquette behind it.
