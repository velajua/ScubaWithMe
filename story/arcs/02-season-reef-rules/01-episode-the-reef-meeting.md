# Episode 1: The Reef Meeting

Lead: Group

Purpose: Establish the reef's rule-making format and show the crew turning fear into boundaries.

## 4-Frame Script

### Panel 1

Visual: Turtle floats calmly in the center of a simple reef clearing while the others gather around.

Text: `Reef meeting. Everyone stay calm.`

### Panel 2

Visual: Grumpy Fish arrives with an irritated face, hovering beside a long strip of seaweed like a complaint list.

Text: `I brought notes. Many notes.`

### Panel 3

Visual: Lionfish poses near a coral edge, inspecting the light as if this is a stage.

Text: `Does this meeting have lighting?`

### Panel 4

Visual: Shark glides behind the group with a deadpan, mildly tired expression.

Text: `Can rule one be no dramatic assumptions?`

## Final Turn

Group comedy: the reef is scared, but organized enough to turn fear into rules.
