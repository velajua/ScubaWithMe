# Episode 8: The Rule Everyone Forgets

Lead: Group

Purpose: Close the arc with the biggest rule: the reef continues without humans.

## 4-Frame Script

### Panel 1

Visual: The crew gathers around Turtle in the reef clearing again, reviewing the rules.

Text: `Look. Do not touch. No chasing.`

### Panel 2

Visual: Grumpy Fish, Lionfish, and Shark argue lightly over the list.

Text: `And no sand weather.`

### Panel 3

Visual: Shark looks thoughtful, drifting just behind the others.

Text: `What rule comes before those?`

### Panel 4

Visual: The reef continues around them with tiny fish, coral, and soft bubbles while Turtle looks outward.

Text: `The reef was busy before anyone arrived.`

## Final Turn

Brand thesis: the reef is not a stage for visitors; it is a living place.
