# Episode 7: Moving Away Means No

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Teach animal body language in one simple, repeatable line.

## 4-Frame Script

### Panel 1

Visual: A small reef fish turns away from a distant diver and begins swimming behind coral.

Text: `It turned away.`

### Panel 2

Visual: The quiet diver lowers the camera instead of following.

Text: `Camera down.`

### Panel 3

Visual: Grumpy Fish squints, confused but interested.

Text: `Did it say something?`

### Panel 4

Visual: Turtle floats beside Grumpy Fish, calm and certain.

Text: `It moved away. That's growth.`

## Final Turn

Tiny ocean truth: behavior is communication.
