# Episode 3: No Chasing

Lead: Shark

Purpose: Explain pursuit as pressure and let Shark read fear in another animal.

## 4-Frame Script

### Panel 1

Visual: A distant diver follows a nervous small fish across the reef with a camera.

Text: `It keeps following.`

### Panel 2

Visual: The small fish zigzags away while Shark watches from the side, serious but not scary.

Text: `Three direction changes.`

### Panel 3

Visual: Shark accidentally drifts between the camera and the small fish, blocking the view.

Text: `Oops. Large body.`

### Panel 4

Visual: The small fish escapes behind coral while Shark looks calm and firm.

Text: `If they speed up, you are not invited.`

## Final Turn

Practical rule: chasing changes curiosity into pressure.
