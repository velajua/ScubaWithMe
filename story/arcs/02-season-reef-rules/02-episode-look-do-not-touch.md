# Episode 2: Look, Do Not Touch

Lead: Lionfish

Purpose: Make the central boundary rule memorable through Lionfish's beauty and personal space.

## 4-Frame Script

### Panel 1

Visual: Lionfish holds a graceful pose in soft blue water beside simple coral.

Text: `Observe the composition.`

### Panel 2

Visual: A small curious fish drifts too close to Lionfish's fins.

Text: `That is... closer than observing.`

### Panel 3

Visual: Lionfish flares dramatically while the small fish backs away.

Text: `The art has boundaries.`

### Panel 4

Visual: Turtle watches gently from nearby as everyone leaves Lionfish some space.

Text: `Beautiful things still get to say no.`

## Final Turn

Respect lesson: admiration does not cancel distance.
