# Season 2: Reef Rules

## Purpose

Turn the reef's fear into clear boundaries. The crew learns that respect is a pattern, not a mood.

Minimum length: 8 episodes.

## Episode 1: The Reef Meeting

Lead: Group

Purpose: Establish the reef's rule-making format.

4-frame outline:

1. Turtle calls a reef meeting.
2. Grumpy Fish brings a long list of complaints.
3. Lionfish asks whether the meeting has lighting.
4. Shark says, "Can rule one be no dramatic assumptions?"

Final turn: group comedy.

## Episode 2: Look, Do Not Touch

Lead: Lionfish

Purpose: Make the central boundary rule memorable.

4-frame outline:

1. Lionfish demonstrates her perfect pose.
2. A small fish gets too close.
3. Lionfish flares and everyone backs up.
4. Turtle says, "Beautiful things still get to say no."

Final turn: respect lesson.

## Episode 3: No Chasing

Lead: Shark

Purpose: Explain pursuit as pressure.

4-frame outline:

1. A diver follows a nervous fish too closely.
2. Shark watches the fish change direction three times.
3. Shark blocks the camera view by accident.
4. Shark says, "If they speed up, you are not invited."

Final turn: practical rule.

## Episode 4: No Sandstorms

Lead: Grumpy Fish

Purpose: Turn a real dive problem into a comic rule.

4-frame outline:

1. Sand drifts into Grumpy Fish's hiding spot.
2. He emerges looking furious.
3. Turtle points at the fin marks.
4. Grumpy Fish says, "Rule three: feet are not weather."

Final turn: funny rule.

## Episode 5: Flash In Sleepy Faces

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Introduce light sensitivity without overexplaining.

4-frame outline:

1. A sleepy reef creature blinks under a bright light.
2. Grumpy Fish complains on its behalf.
3. Turtle watches the creature retreat.
4. Turtle says, "A good look should not cost someone their calm."

Final turn: quiet boundary.

## Episode 6: Distance Is Respect

Lead: Lionfish

Purpose: Repeat the core brand rule in a more graceful way.

4-frame outline:

1. Lionfish poses in open water.
2. The quiet diver stops at a distance.
3. Lionfish notices and stays.
4. Lionfish says, "At last. Admiration with a horizon."

Final turn: elegant humor.

## Episode 7: Moving Away Means No

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Teach animal body language.

4-frame outline:

1. A small fish turns away from the diver.
2. The quiet diver lowers the camera.
3. Grumpy Fish asks if the fish said something.
4. Turtle says, "It moved away. That's growth."

Final turn: tiny ocean truth.

## Episode 8: The Rule Everyone Forgets

Lead: Group

Purpose: Close with the biggest rule: the reef continues without humans.

4-frame outline:

1. The crew reviews the reef rules.
2. Everyone argues over wording.
3. Shark asks what rule comes before all the others.
4. Turtle says, "The reef was busy before anyone arrived."

Final turn: brand thesis.
