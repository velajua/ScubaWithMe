# Arc: Current Adventure

## Premise

A strong current carries one character away from the familiar reef. What begins as a small drift turns into a wider look at the underwater world.

## Main Uses

- adventure
- new habitats
- side character introductions
- visual variety
- teaching that calm and passive are not the same

## Plot Points

- A current picks up unexpectedly.
- Turtle rides it farther than planned.
- Familiar coral disappears.
- Jellyfish appears, calm and drifting.
- Turtle learns that being calm does not mean giving up control.
- The group searches for Turtle.
- Shark reads the current better than expected.
- Grumpy Fish pretends not to be worried.
- Lionfish insists search parties need better lighting.
- The route home gets a poetic name.

## Possible Ending

Turtle returns with a new route name and a larger sense of the reef.

## 8-Episode Outline

### Episode 1: Current Business

Lead: Grumpy Fish

4-frame outline:

1. The water starts pulling harder than usual.
2. Grumpy Fish objects to the schedule.
3. Turtle says the current has its own errands.
4. Shark says, "This is current business."

Final turn: the current becomes a recurring force.

### Episode 2: The Long Drift

Lead: Turtle

4-frame outline:

1. Turtle follows a gentle pull past the home coral.
2. The reef sounds fade behind him.
3. Familiar landmarks disappear.
4. Turtle says, "The long way just got longer."

Final turn: adventure begins.

### Episode 3: No Panic, Probably

Lead: Grumpy Fish

4-frame outline:

1. The crew realizes Turtle has not returned.
2. Grumpy Fish says no one is panicking.
3. A school of fish panics behind him.
4. Grumpy Fish says, "That is unrelated background panic."

Final turn: comic worry.

### Episode 4: Jellyfish Knows Nothing And Everything

Lead: Turtle

4-frame outline:

1. Turtle meets Jellyfish drifting calmly.
2. Turtle asks where the current leads.
3. Jellyfish says, "Somewhere that was already waiting."
4. Turtle says, "That is not a map, but it is something."

Final turn: dreamy guidance.

### Episode 5: Shark Reads The Water

Lead: Shark

4-frame outline:

1. Shark studies sand movement and suspended particles.
2. Lionfish asks if this is a dramatic pause.
3. Shark points toward the channel.
4. Shark says, "The water leaves handwriting."

Final turn: Shark competence.

### Episode 6: The Shadow Garden

Lead: Turtle

4-frame outline:

1. Turtle reaches a darker coral edge.
2. Small animals tuck into crevices.
3. Turtle slows to avoid disturbing them.
4. Turtle names it "the shadow garden."

Final turn: place-making.

### Episode 7: Found

Lead: Group

4-frame outline:

1. Shark leads the crew toward the darker coral.
2. Grumpy Fish says this rescue needs snacks.
3. Turtle appears from behind the coral.
4. Lionfish says, "The lighting was terrible, but the entrance was strong."

Final turn: reunion.

### Episode 8: More Ocean

Lead: Turtle

4-frame outline:

1. The crew returns home.
2. The reef feels smaller for a moment.
3. Grumpy Fish asks if the current broke home.
4. Turtle says, "No. It showed us more ocean."

Final turn: expansion and wonder.
