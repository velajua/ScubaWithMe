# Season 5: Moving Houses

## Purpose

Use a storm and changed hiding places to explore home, territory, comfort, and community.

Minimum length: 8 episodes.

## Episode 1: After The Storm

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Show the reef changed.

4-frame outline:

1. The crew returns after rough water.
2. Sand has shifted around familiar coral.
3. Grumpy Fish stares at his filled-in hiding place.
4. Turtle says, "The reef moved while we were listening to the storm."

Final turn: quiet change.

## Episode 2: My Hole Is Gone

Lead: Grumpy Fish

Purpose: Make the loss personal and funny.

4-frame outline:

1. Grumpy Fish tries to enter his old spot.
2. Sand blocks the entrance.
3. Shark asks if he has tried being flexible.
4. Grumpy Fish says, "I am grieving architecture."

Final turn: comic emotion.

## Episode 3: Shell With Character

Lead: Hermit Crab

Purpose: Introduce Hermit Crab's housing problem.

4-frame outline:

1. Hermit Crab drags a cracked shell.
2. Turtle asks if it fits.
3. Hermit Crab says it has character.
4. The shell wobbles and Hermit Crab adds, "Unfortunately, the character is damp."

Final turn: side character hook.

## Episode 4: Shark's Suggestions

Lead: Shark

Purpose: Show Shark trying to help badly.

4-frame outline:

1. Shark offers a huge cave as a new home.
2. Grumpy Fish says it has excellent echo and no privacy.
3. Hermit Crab says it is more weather system than house.
4. Shark says, "I was thinking spacious."

Final turn: scale mismatch.

## Episode 5: Lionfish Judges Lighting

Lead: Lionfish

Purpose: Bring personality into practical decisions.

4-frame outline:

1. The crew tours possible hiding spots.
2. Lionfish rejects one for bad lighting.
3. Grumpy Fish says he is not opening a restaurant.
4. Lionfish says, "Every home is a statement."

Final turn: character comedy.

## Episode 6: Tiny Goby's Kingdom

Lead: Tiny Goby

Purpose: Add territorial comedy.

4-frame outline:

1. Grumpy Fish finds a promising rock.
2. Tiny Goby appears and declares it occupied.
3. Grumpy Fish says he cannot even see the kingdom.
4. Tiny Goby says, "That is because it is exclusive."

Final turn: small-scale world-building.

## Episode 7: The Not-Pretty Home

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Shift from aesthetics to function.

4-frame outline:

1. The crew finds a plain, shaded spot.
2. Lionfish is unimpressed.
3. Grumpy Fish tries it and relaxes.
4. Turtle says, "Pretty is nice. Safe is home."

Final turn: lesson.

## Episode 8: Neighbors

Lead: Group

Purpose: Close the arc with community.

4-frame outline:

1. Hermit Crab settles into a better shell.
2. Grumpy Fish finds a new hiding place nearby.
3. Shark circles once to check the current.
4. Turtle says, "Home is shelter, memory, and neighbors."

Final turn: emotional close.
