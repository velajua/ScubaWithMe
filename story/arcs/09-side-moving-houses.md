# Arc: Moving Houses

## Premise

A storm changes the reef's small hiding spots and forces several creatures to find new homes.

## Main Uses

- roommate-style comedy
- home and territory
- Hermit Crab introduction
- practical reef problem-solving

## Plot Points

- A storm rearranges sand and loose coral.
- Grumpy Fish's favorite hiding spot fills in.
- Hermit Crab needs a new shell.
- Turtle organizes a calm search.
- Shark suggests huge caves no one else can use.
- Lionfish judges every option by lighting.
- Tiny Goby claims every rock is already occupied.
- The group finds several possible homes.
- The best home is not the prettiest one.

## Possible Ending

Home becomes shelter, memory, and neighbors.

## 8-Episode Outline

### Episode 1: The Reef Rearranged Itself

Lead: Turtle

4-frame outline:

1. The crew returns after rough water.
2. Sand and loose coral have shifted.
3. Grumpy Fish asks who moved the furniture.
4. Turtle says, "The storm did not ask for the floor plan."

Final turn: changed world.

### Episode 2: Grumpy Fish Loses His Spot

Lead: Grumpy Fish

4-frame outline:

1. Grumpy Fish tries to enter his old hiding place.
2. The entrance is blocked by sand.
3. Shark suggests a new place.
4. Grumpy Fish says, "I had the old place broken in emotionally."

Final turn: comic grief.

### Episode 3: Hermit Crab's Search

Lead: Hermit Crab

4-frame outline:

1. Hermit Crab drags a shell that does not fit.
2. Turtle offers to help search.
3. Hermit Crab says finding a home is serious business.
4. Lionfish says, "Finally, someone respects staging."

Final turn: practical quest.

### Episode 4: Too Big, Too Loud

Lead: Shark

4-frame outline:

1. Shark presents a huge cave.
2. His voice echoes through it.
3. Grumpy Fish says the cave has no emotional corners.
4. Shark says, "I may have overestimated everyone."

Final turn: scale joke.

### Episode 5: Lighting Standards

Lead: Lionfish

4-frame outline:

1. Lionfish rates several possible homes.
2. She rejects one for shadows and one for bad angles.
3. Grumpy Fish says he is trying to live, not model.
4. Lionfish says, "Those are not opposites."

Final turn: personality comedy.

### Episode 6: Tiny Goby's Kingdom

Lead: Tiny Goby

4-frame outline:

1. Grumpy Fish finds a perfect small rock.
2. Tiny Goby appears and claims it.
3. Grumpy Fish says the kingdom is barely visible.
4. Tiny Goby says, "Then squint with respect."

Final turn: territorial humor.

### Episode 7: The Plain Place

Lead: Turtle

4-frame outline:

1. Turtle finds a plain shaded space.
2. Grumpy Fish tries it reluctantly.
3. The current passes gently around it.
4. Turtle says, "A good home does not need applause."

Final turn: quiet lesson.

### Episode 8: New Neighbors

Lead: Group

4-frame outline:

1. Hermit Crab settles into a better shell.
2. Grumpy Fish settles nearby.
3. Shark checks the open-water side.
4. Turtle says, "Home is what shelters you and who notices when it changes."

Final turn: emotional close.
