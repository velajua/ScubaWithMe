# Episode 4: Admire From There

Lead: Lionfish

Purpose: Introduce beauty and boundaries.

## 4-Frame Script

### Panel 1

Visual: Lionfish glides into a patch of nice underwater light.

Text: `The lighting has arrived.`

### Panel 2

Visual: The distant diver pauses and raises a camera from far away.

Text: `Finally. An audience.`

### Panel 3

Visual: The diver drifts slightly closer. Lionfish's expression changes from proud to stern.

Text: `Careful.`

### Panel 4

Visual: The diver backs away respectfully. Lionfish relaxes and poses again.

Text: `Admire from there.`

## Final Turn

Distance is respect.
