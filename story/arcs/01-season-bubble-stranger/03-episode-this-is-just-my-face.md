# Episode 3: This Is Just My Face

Lead: Shark

Purpose: Introduce Shark as misunderstood.

## 4-Frame Script

### Panel 1

Visual: Shark sees the distant diver's camera turn toward him from above.

Text: `Oh no. The eye found me.`

### Panel 2

Visual: Shark tries to duck behind coral, but his body is too large to hide.

Text: `Subtle. Very subtle.`

### Panel 3

Visual: Grumpy Fish watches Shark failing to hide.

Text: `Hiding is not your strongest skill.`

### Panel 4

Visual: Shark looks deadpan and mildly offended.

Text: `*Bombastic-Side-Eye*`

## Final Turn

Humor plus empathy: Shark looks scary, but his deadpan side-eye makes the assumption feel ridiculous.
