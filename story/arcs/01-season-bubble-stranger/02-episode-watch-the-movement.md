# Episode 2: Watch The Movement

Lead: Turtle

Purpose: Establish Turtle's observation-first role.

## 4-Frame Script

### Panel 1

Visual: A distant diver shape appears far above the reef, mostly a simple silhouette with bubbles. The reef animals are still small in the foreground.

Text: `There. Above the coral.`

### Panel 2

Visual: Grumpy Fish points or glares upward, annoyed and suspicious.

Text: `Bubble machine. Called it.`

### Panel 3

Visual: Turtle calmly watches the distant diver, noticing that the diver is hovering instead of chasing anything.

Text: `It is not chasing.`

### Panel 4

Visual: Turtle and Grumpy Fish watch the diver from the reef. The diver remains distant and gentle. Grumpy Fish is still skeptical.

Text: `Shape tells one story. Movement tells another.`

## Final Turn

Quiet lesson: Turtle teaches that behavior matters more than first impressions.
