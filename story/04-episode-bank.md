# Episode Bank

## Repeatable 4-Frame Formula

Frame 1: Set the tiny problem.

Frame 2: Character reacts in their signature way.

Frame 3: Another character complicates or reframes it.

Frame 4: Land the joke, lesson, or wonder.

## Diver Encounter Formula

1. A diver enters the reef.
2. A character misreads what the diver is doing.
3. Turtle suggests watching before reacting.
4. The reef turns the moment into a rule, joke, or field note.

## Solo Adventure Formula

1. One character goes off alone.
2. They encounter a current, cave, strange animal, or object.
3. Their personality creates the problem.
4. Someone or something reframes it.
5. The adventure returns to the group with a new lesson.

## Neighborhood Problem Formula

1. The reef has a practical problem.
2. The crew solves it badly first.
3. A side character complicates it.
4. Turtle, Shark, Grumpy Fish, or Lionfish reframes it.
5. The reef feels more lived-in afterward.

## Finished Seed: First Drift

1. Turtle: "Welcome below."
2. Grumpy Fish: "Keep your fins to yourself."
3. Shark drifts past: "And your dramatic assumptions."
4. Turtle: "The reef is friendlier when you move gently."

## Finished Seed: No Coffee, No Talkie

1. Grumpy Fish stares at viewer.
2. Turtle: "Morning."
3. Grumpy Fish: "I have been awake for twelve seconds."
4. Caption: "Some reef residents need a little space."

## Finished Seed: Side-Eye Patrol

1. Shark enters frame looking intense.
2. Grumpy Fish: "Why do you always look like that?"
3. Shark: "This is just my face."
4. Turtle: "Not every serious face is a threat."

## Finished Seed: Look, Don't Touch

1. Lionfish poses dramatically.
2. Shark: "You know you are very extra, right?"
3. Lionfish: "I prefer visually important."
4. Turtle: "Beautiful things still deserve distance."

## Finished Seed: Reef Meeting

1. Turtle: "Today's rule: calm movement."
2. Shark: "No panic."
3. Lionfish: "No touching the art."
4. Grumpy Fish: "And no flash photography in my face."

## Additional 4-Frame Seeds

- A diver appears above the reef. Grumpy Fish calls them bubble machines. Turtle notices this one moves slowly. Shark says, "Suspiciously polite."
- Lionfish poses under blue light. Grumpy Fish asks if she is done. Lionfish says beauty requires patience. Turtle adds, "And distance."
- Shark hides behind coral and fails because he is too large. Grumpy Fish says subtlety has left the reef. Shark says, "I am working with the body I was given."
- Turtle takes the long route around a coral head. Grumpy Fish complains. Turtle points out three tiny creatures everyone would have missed.
- A jellyfish drifts through the reef. Everyone asks where it is going. Jellyfish says the water has already made plans.
- Hermit Crab rejects six shells. Shark offers a cave. Hermit Crab says, "That is not a home. That is a weather event."
- Cleaner Shrimp hears every argument during cleaning. Grumpy Fish calls it gossip. Cleaner Shrimp calls it maintenance.
- A diver's camera light catches Lionfish. Lionfish enjoys it until it gets too close. Turtle says attention is not the same as respect.
- Grumpy Fish photobombs the calm diver's photo. He pretends it was an accident. The next day he asks whether the angle was flattering.
- Shark sees a boat shadow. Everyone expects him to be brave. He admits loud shadows feel different from below.
