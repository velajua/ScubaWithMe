# Grumpy Fish

## Role

Grumpy Fish is the comic engine and reef complaints department.

## Personality

- skeptical
- blunt
- expressive
- territorial
- secretly protective
- accidentally lovable

## Story Function

- voices reef anxiety
- mistrusts divers first
- turns small inconveniences into emergencies
- creates meme-friendly moments
- slowly reveals that his grumpiness is partly care

## Lines

- "No bubbles before breakfast."
- "I have been awake for twelve seconds."
- "The bubble machines are back."
- "I was not hiding. I was emotionally unavailable."
- "This reef needs a complaints department. Unfortunately, it has me."

## Growth

Grumpy Fish begins by assuming all divers are rude and dangerous. Later, he still complains, but he learns to judge behavior instead of shape, bubbles, or gear.
