# Turtle

## Role

Turtle is the calm guide and emotional center of `Below With Me`.

## Personality

- patient
- warm
- observant
- cautious but curious
- slow, never sleepy
- notices small details

## Story Function

- invites the viewer into the reef
- asks others to watch before reacting
- turns panic into observation
- names routes and places poetically
- carries the ScubaWithMe feeling: "come see this with me"

## Lines

- "Slow down. Look closer."
- "Welcome below."
- "The reef tells you more when you stop rushing."
- "Curiosity and caution can swim together."
- "The long way usually shows you more."

## Growth

Turtle starts curious about divers but cautious. Over time, Turtle learns that calmness still needs boundaries and that being gentle does not mean being passive.
