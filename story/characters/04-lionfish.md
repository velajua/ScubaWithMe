# Lionfish

## Role

Lionfish is the dramatic, elegant boundary character.

## Personality

- theatrical
- proud
- visually stylish
- precise
- attention-loving but guarded
- beautiful from a distance

## Story Function

- teaches that attention is not respect
- carries "look, do not touch"
- brings visual drama to posts
- can be funny without losing the boundary message

## Lines

- "Admire from a distance."
- "I prefer visually important."
- "Beauty is not an invitation."
- "The lighting has finally understood me."
- "No touching the art."

## Growth

Lionfish enjoys admiration at first, but a too-close diver makes her withdraw. She learns to define attention on her own terms, and the reef learns that distance can be a kind of respect.
