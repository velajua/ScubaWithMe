# Shark

## Role

Shark is the misunderstood local.

## Personality

- calm
- dry
- observant
- intimidating by accident
- emotionally sharper than others expect
- protective in quiet ways

## Story Function

- challenges fear-based myths
- carries the "not every serious face is a threat" theme
- understands open water, shadows, and current
- gives good advice in a scary silhouette

## Lines

- "This is just my face."
- "I am literally just swimming."
- "Not every serious face is a threat."
- "I can be calm and still have teeth."
- "I am not chasing. I am commuting."

## Growth

Shark starts tired of being feared and photographed like a monster. Over time, Shark learns that being seen calmly can feel different from being hunted by attention.
