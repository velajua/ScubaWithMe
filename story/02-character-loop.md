# Ensemble Character Loop

The story should orbit around each main character instead of having only one permanent lead.

## Weekly Loop

- Day 1: Turtle lead.
- Day 2: Grumpy Fish lead.
- Day 3: Shark lead.
- Day 4: Lionfish lead.
- Day 5: Group reef moment.
- Day 6: Side character, adventure, or current episode.
- Day 7: quiet field-note style post.

## Why This Works

- Turtle keeps the brand warm and grounded.
- Grumpy Fish creates attention and shareable humor.
- Shark carries the misunderstood-animal theme.
- Lionfish carries beauty, distance, and visual drama.
- Group episodes make the reef feel like a neighborhood.
- Side characters keep the feed from feeling repetitive.

## Lead Character Jobs

### Turtle

Use Turtle when the episode needs:

- calm guidance
- observation
- patience
- emotional resolution
- "come see this with me" energy

### Grumpy Fish

Use Grumpy Fish when the episode needs:

- fast humor
- skepticism
- a blunt reaction
- a complaint
- a secretly protective turn

### Shark

Use Shark when the episode needs:

- misunderstood identity
- fear versus reality
- calm strength
- open water
- a serious silhouette with a gentle line

### Lionfish

Use Lionfish when the episode needs:

- visual drama
- boundaries
- attention versus respect
- elegance
- a clear "look, do not touch" message

### Group

Use the group when the episode needs:

- reef rules
- neighborhood comedy
- meetings
- argument
- a shared lesson
